Why host on UseMyTech?

No matter what kind of technology you have to share, UseMyTech makes it simple and secure to rent out your tech. You’re in full control of your availability, prices, and rules.

UseMyTech protects hosts

To keep you and your techs afe, we cover every rental with $1M USD in property damage protection and another $1M USD in insurance against accidents.
